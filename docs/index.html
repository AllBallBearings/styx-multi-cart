Why Styx

Your carts, your way

Amazon gives you one cart. Styx gives you as many as you need — and keeps them under your control.

🛒

Save named carts

Snapshot your active Amazon cart under any name you choose — "Birthday gifts," "Office supplies," "Wishlist." Free for 2 saved carts, or go Premium for up to 20.

🔁

Switch with one click

Swap your live cart for any saved cart instantly. "Save & clear" snapshots your current cart and empties it so you can start fresh.

🔒

100% local & private

Everything lives in chrome.storage.local on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking — your carts never leave your browser.

How it works

From cart chaos to one click

A few screens from the actual extension in action on amazon.com.

Step 1

Your carts, at a glance

Open the side panel next to your Amazon cart to see every saved cart — name, item count, and total — without leaving the page.

Step 2

Switch carts without losing your place

Pick a saved cart and Styx asks for confirmation before swapping it in — your current cart is replaced safely, with no surprises.

Step 3

Add to cart, your way

Styx steps in right when you click Add to Cart on Amazon, so new items can be routed to the saved cart you're building — not just your live cart.

Step 4

Pick exactly which cart gets the item

Choose any saved cart from the picker and the item is filed there — perfect for building out gift lists or project orders while you keep browsing.
